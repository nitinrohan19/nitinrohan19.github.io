Analysis of CyrptoCurrencies
Data Analytics course under Prof. Hardeep Johar, Columbia University
Report / Code

Analyzed different cryptocurrencies by market cap and gain % and derived heat map from their correlation matrix. Used Natural Language Processing (NLP) to do text mining and then perform sentiment analysis to gather feedback. Integrated GoogleTrends API to gain insights about the distribution of searches of topics and identify trends over time.
